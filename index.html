Welcome to the Transport Transition Map Europe!

This interactive map offers a comprehensive exploration of the dynamic transportation landscape unfolding across Europe. At its core, the map serves as a visual narrative of the continent's ongoing transition towards sustainable transportation practices and the collective effort to combat climate change.

Embark on a journey through this interactive platform to gain valuable insights into the multifaceted strategies and initiatives driving Europe's transportation evolution. From the bustling streets of urban centers to the serene countryside roads, every aspect of Europe's transportation network is meticulously mapped out for your exploration.

Delve into the intricacies of various measures meticulously designed to promote sustainable mobility and reduce carbon emissions. Whether it's the expansion of eco-friendly public transit systems, the proliferation of cycling lanes and pedestrian-friendly zones, or the adoption of innovative technologies in transportation, this map encapsulates the diversity of efforts underway.

Utilize the dropdown menus meticulously curated in the sidebar to navigate through different dimensions of the transportation transition. Explore the status of initiatives, ranging from those in the planning stages to those already making significant strides towards implementation. Engage with specific measures aimed at reshaping the transportation landscape, each contributing to the collective goal of building a greener, more sustainable future for generations to come.
